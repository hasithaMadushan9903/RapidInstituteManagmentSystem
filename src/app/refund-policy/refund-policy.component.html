Refund Policy

Effective Date: 01 January 2026

EduGrowa provides digital tutoring services. Due to the nature of these services, the following refund rules apply:

1. Non-Refundable Payments

Once access to online classes, recordings, or materials is granted, payments are non-refundable.

2. Technical Issues

If a technical issue from our side prevents access to paid services, we may offer a refund or alternative class access after review.

3. Duplicate Payments

Duplicate or accidental payments will be refunded after verification.

4. Refund Requests

All refund requests must be submitted within 7 days of payment with valid proof of transaction.

5. Refund Method

Approved refunds will be processed via PayHere to the original payment method.
